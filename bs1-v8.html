Your questions, answered.

How much does it cost?

Engagements start in the mid-four figures and go up based on length and access level. It's a high-touch engagement designed to build the dating infrastructure and skillset you keep for life.

Why are some client testimonials blurred or anonymous?

Because most of the men we work with are founders, executives, and high‑earning professionals who don't want their dating life searchable online. Privacy is part of the service: everyone signs a confidentiality agreement, and we only show what they're comfortable sharing, which is why you see anonymized case studies. The data points, ages, professions, and results, are all real. We just protect identities. If you end up inside Atlas, you get the same level of discretion.

I'm busy. How much time will this take, and what's required?

Most clients spend about 5 focused hours per week, including time actually spent on dates. We do ~80% of the heavy lifting, but you still have to send the messages, show up to dates, and follow the gameplan. Atlas is built for busy men, not passive ones.

Isn't this pickup?

No. Atlas is not about memorizing lines, manipulating women, or becoming someone you're not. It's a structured advisory process where we upgrade how you look, how you present online, how you meet women, and how you interact with the women you like most. The goal is simple: make your dating life as intentional and effective as the rest of your life.

Can't I just do this myself?

Yes, you can certainly try. But doing it alone usually means months or years of trial and error with no clear feedback and no guarantee of success. All while you waste chances with women you actually liked because your photos, texting, dates, or approaches were lacking. Inside Atlas, we rebuild your profiles, fix your messaging, and review the actual conversations, dates, and approaches you're having so you know exactly what to adjust before you waste more time or lose more opportunities.

Why not just hire a matchmaker?

A high‑end matchmaker will happily take between $30K and $50K to introduce you to a recycled pool of women who have already been on dates with tons of other wealthy men (aka gold diggers). Their job ends at “here’s her profile / here’s the dinner reservation.” If your matchmaker disappeared tomorrow, how many great dates could you still create on your own? That’s the difference. When the intros stop, your results stop. Atlas builds your ability to generate your own options through apps, Instagram, and real life so you don’t need a gatekeeper to meet high‑quality women.
